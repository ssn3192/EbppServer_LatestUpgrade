Thank you for using County of Hawaii's online payment service. Administrative fees associated with online payments will be passed on to you in the form of an additional fee
by the online vendor.The fee will be disclosed to you when you confirm the payment.

Administrative fees apply to each online water payment processed. There is a $2.50 fee for Credit Card payments with a $5,000 limit per payment transaction. For
electronic check payments drawn on US Banks there is a fee of $3.50 per payment transaction. Electronic check payments from Non-US Banks are not allowed.

Returned Payments: If your electronic check or credit card payment is returned or disputed for any reason you are responsible for the full amount of the payment (including
the administrative fees) plus $25.00.

payment to your water account. Your payment may not appear on your next water bill and a late penalty may be charged to your account if the payment due date on your last
